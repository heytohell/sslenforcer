SSLE uses regular expressions for rules.
Rules should not include the protocol (http://, https://) but may include the URI.
The anchors (^, $) are not implicit, use them as required.
Match all subdomains and URIs: ^.*\.wikipedia\.org
Match the fqdn its root URI only: ^www\.google\.com/$
Match a specific URL: ^www\.rogers\.com/web/Rogers\.portal$
Warning: Options are not encrypted in storage. Do not add URLs with sensitive information (usernames, passwords, session IDs).
